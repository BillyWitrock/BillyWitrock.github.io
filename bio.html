Short Biography of Billy Witrock

Billy was born Feburary 20th 1998 to proud parents, Andrew and Diane. He grew up in the small village of Port Jefferson, where he attended the local elementry, middle, and high schools. During his time in Port Jeff, he had many outstanding achievements. These achievements include but are not limited to bowling a record high school of 175 to placing second in the annual school-wide walk-run in the first grade division. He now attends Tufts University and hopes to acheive more acheivements of equal caliber.
